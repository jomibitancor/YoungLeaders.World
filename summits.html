Summits

All of our energy, talent, growth and awesomeness comes together in our Summits. There is no question, our summits are high energy and high impact. Young leaders come together to learn meet and share about what leadership means to them. Summits feature 4-5 speakers throughout the day, provide students with thought provoking leadership questions, learning partners to share their thoughts with and clear, personalized next steps to take after Summit is done. You won't leave the same way you came in.
