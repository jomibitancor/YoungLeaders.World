Leader's Journey

At YoungLeaders.World we work with young people ages 14-20 to help them understand how to lead their lives. Our team has developed the 5 step leaders journey. This helps indiviudals, teams, organizations and schools make changes to help them reach their leadership goals.

The 5 pillars of the Leader's Journey include

Vision

Where are you going?

Values

What lines won't you cross?

Mission

What will keep you going?

Strengths

What will you bring with you?

Barriers

What will you leave behind?

A full course to work through the Leader's Journey is coming in the Spring of 2020.
