We collaborate with young people around the world to help them discover life leadership. YL.W is dedicated to helping young people, ages 13-21, understand how to develop a leader mindset. How to think like a leader, make decisions like a leader and articulate your goals. We believe leadership is a mindset, not a status.

Things to know about the VISION2020 broadcast

  1. Schools who have already paid for the conference will be reimbursed in the coming weeks. Schools who are already registered for the conference will automatically be registered for the broadcast.
  2. Schools are encouraged to invite up to 250 students to watch the conference from their gym, theatre or an unlimited amount of students in their individual classrooms. We want as many students to hear the leadership messaging as possible.
